E-COMMERCE WEB APP

ABSTRACT

This web application is mainly created for the peoples and vendors and to sell their product through this site which is the fundamental feature of this site.We all know that in this pandemic having an ecommerce is soo crucial. In the Ecommerce app there are 3 types of users , Admin , Vendor and Customer . The Admin can add the vendors , view the products , orders and manage customers . The vendor can login with the account credentials and they can add product ,manage product , and manage the orders . The customers can login with gmail and password and select the vendors or products , add the products to cart and check out them using different types of payment options . Customers can also view the previous orders.
